The Healthcare System Needs Workers. Communities Need Jobs.

Right now, two urgent problems exist side by side — and the gap between them is solvable.

Healthcare is short-staffed.

Hospitals, clinics, and telehealth providers across the country need patient care coordinators, schedulers, and support staff — and they need them now. Remote healthcare roles are growing fast, but the talent pipeline hasn't kept up.

Too many people are underemployed or out of work.

Millions of capable, motivated individuals — especially in low-income communities — are stuck in unstable jobs or long-term unemployment. Traditional training programs take months, cost too much, and often don't lead directly to a paycheck.

The gap is solvable.

But only if we move faster, train smarter, and focus on what actually gets people hired. That's where Digital Horizon Project comes in.

AI-Powered Training. Real Jobs. Real Speed.

Digital Horizon Project is a nonprofit workforce engine that uses artificial intelligence to dramatically compress the path from unemployment to employment in healthcare.

  • Short, focused training Our core program takes just 30–45 minutes per module, built around the exact tasks employers ask for every day.
  • AI as a learning partner Trainees practice with AI-generated roleplays, draft real workplace communications, and get instant feedback on their skills.
  • Built for remote work We prepare candidates for telehealth support, care coordination, and patient access roles that can be done from home.
  • Job-ready, not just trained Every lesson connects directly to what employers need: scheduling, insurance verification, phone communication, EHR basics, and professional writing.

Most trainees are ready to apply for jobs in 2–4 weeks.

Why We Exist

Digital Horizon Project was founded on a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunity isn't.

We exist to close that gap — using the best tools available, including artificial intelligence, to help people move from underemployment into meaningful, stable healthcare careers.

We are not a traditional school. We are not a bootcamp. We are a workforce engine — built for speed, designed for outcomes, and committed to the idea that the best way to fight poverty is to get people paychecks.

Every decision we make is guided by one question: Does this get someone hired faster?

If the answer is yes, we do it. If not, we rethink it.

Employment is the product. AI is the accelerator. People are the point.
